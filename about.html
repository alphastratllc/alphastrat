About

Dr. Surendra Batra has over 20 years of experience researching with collagen, and has been integral in bringing many collagen-based medical products to market for different leading entities. Dr. Batra is considered to be a leading collagen expert. During this time, he has focused on multiple collagen-based medical applications.

Experience

  • Founder and chief research scientist focused on developing collagen based technologies and products for implants and surgeries including neurological, orthopedic, dental and general surgical applications.
  • Partnering with leading biomedical corporations to license and market products and processes developed in-house.
  • Currently collaborating with biomedical firms to develop collagen based products for use in theoral surgery, dental and oral/maxillofacial (OMS) market.
