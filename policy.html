Policy Dialogues

Robust state and local policies are crucial for success in key areas like K-12 education, energy, and housing. However, these critical issues often lack the attention and rigorous analysis they deserve, with the narrative frequently dominated by loud voices or vested interests. This leads to suboptimal policy decisions.

Our objective is to bring clarity and transparency to critical issues. We strive to cut through the noise and focus on the core substance of policies, facilitating meaningful dialogue between elected officials, citizens, and policymakers. Our mission is to deliver clear, impartial information and advocate for policy solutions that serve the best interests of all.

Issues:

  1. Energy: Connecticut faces the paradox of having some of the highest electricity prices in the nation, despite having access to low-cost electricity generation. Despite a decade of investment in renewable energy and substantial subsidies, renewable sources still account for less than 10% of our energy mix. This raises critical questions: Why are costs so high? Why haven't our renewable energy goals been met? We want to bring forth policies which lead to greater affordability, sustainability, and reliability.
