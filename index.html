Mission Statement:

The Citizens Policy Forum of CT empowers residents through informed dialogue and active civic engagement. Our mission is to:

  1. Educate: Keep citizens informed about key policies through emails, social media, and events.
  2. Facilitate Dialogue: Connect the community with elected officials to discuss critical issues.
  3. Research: Partner with experts to conduct unbiased research and offer evidence-based solutions
  4. Advocate: Champion effective policies that address residents’ needs and ensure government accountability.
  5. Promote Participation: Encourage broader citizen involvement in the democratic process
    We invite you to be part of our mission to strengthen Connecticut's democracy and improve our community. Explore our policy papers, participate in our events, and contribute to shaping a better future for all.
